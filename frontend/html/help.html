How to Use EcoGram

Login

To access your EcoGram account:

  • Navigate to the Login page.
  • Enter your username and password in the provided fields.
  • Click the Sign In button to proceed.
  • If you donâ€™t have an account, click on the Sign Up link to register.

Sign Up

To create a new account:

  • Go to the Sign Up page.
  • Fill in your details including:
    • Full Name
    • Email Address
    • Username
    • Password
  • Click the Sign Up button to complete registration.
  • You will be redirected to the Login page after successful registration.

Home

The home page displays your personalized feed:

  • View the latest posts from users you follow.
  • Interact with posts by:
    • Clicking Like to show your appreciation.
    • Adding a Comment to engage with the post.
  • Explore Suggested Groups in the sidebar to join communities.

Profile

Your profile page allows you to manage your account:

  • View and edit your username, bio, and profile picture.
  • Access tabs to:
    • Posts: View all your posts.
    • Liked: See posts you've liked.
    • Settings: Update your account information.
  • Create new posts by uploading an image and writing a caption.

Chats

The chats page allows you to connect with other users:

  • Select a contact from the sidebar to open a conversation.
  • Type your message in the input field and click Send.
  • View previous messages in the chat history.
  • Switch between different conversations using the chat list.

Groups

Join eco-friendly groups and participate in discussions:

  • Browse through the available groups on the Groups page.
  • Click the Join button on a group to become a member.
  • Engage in group activities and discussions to stay active in the community.

Challenges

Participate in exciting challenges and earn rewards:

  • Explore available challenges on the Challenges page.
  • View details of each challenge, including the description, rewards, and deadline.
  • Create posts related to challenges by including the challenge hashtag.
  • Flip challenge cards to view posts associated with the challenge.
