How to Sign Up

To create an EcoGram account:

  • Enter your full name in the Full Name field.
  • Provide a valid email address in the Email field.
  • Choose a username and enter it in the Username field.
  • Set a strong password and enter it in the Password field.
  • Click the Sign Up button to create your account.
